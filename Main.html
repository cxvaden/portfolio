The Natural State




The Arkansas Movie Database was created to highlight the beauty of Arkansas. Arkansas isn't known for it's film industry but more for it's nature. When you watch a movie with people from Arkansas, you become one of the characters. You share their stories, their dreams, their love and their reality. When you watch a movie is set in a location you are failar with, it allows for an even deeper connection. This webpage shares the films and people from my home state, Arkansas. I hope you'll enjoy connecting with the characters and stories that share Arkansas as their stage
